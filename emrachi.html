Overview

Emrachi is located above the Aidec Desert. The capital city of Emrachi is Trismuth. Their patron god is Fi, the god of Fire. Emrachi's climate is still quite dry and arid, but they have more access to water than Secium. Their capital city is frequented by tourists because it lies on the gulf of three kingdoms, which connects Kevroc, Emrachi, and Levania.
