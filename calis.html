Overview

Calis is a village on the edge of the Calis cove. It is separated from the Aidec desert by the Cails Cliffs. The people there are very secluded, and they rely on the cliffs and the cove to survive. Their patron god is Fi, who they learned from Emrachi, the first country to discover them. Calis is secluded, but the people are welcoming and hospitable. Calis has one inn, for visitors. Calis is quickly becoming a popular tourist destination for nobles and the upper class. Emrachians are the most prominent tourists there. Calis is seen as a peaceful destination for people who want to escape their busy everyday lives.
