Overview

Kevroc is the northern-most kingdom in Faymere. Their capital is Kolzi, and it lies in the leftmost side of Kevroc. Their patron god is Ki, the god of the snow. Due to the cold climate, Kevroc's only trees are evergreen trees. They import most of their foods, as they are almost stuck in an eternal winter. However, they have learned to embrace the snow, and they see the eternal winter as a blessing. The Kevrians can control the intensity of the snow, so they are not as impacted by the constant snowfall as someone who is not native to Kevroc would be.
