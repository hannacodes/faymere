Overview

Glimnara, due to the cloud forest, is a very air oriented country. Their patron god is Vy, the god of the air. The main job amongst civilians is foragers or lumberjacks. They utilize the resources in their area, such as the many trees in the area. Because of their elemental magic, the Glimnarians are the only people in Faymere who can, essentially, fly. They use this power to harvest fruit that grows on the tall trees of Glimnara. The safest way to reach the capital city of Akzil is by water, or by following the river.
