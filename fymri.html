Overview

Fymri, also known as the Isle of Fymri, is an island located in the top left of the world of Faymere. The only way to reach there is across the ocean. Fymri's capital city is Saltu. Fymri's patron god is El, the god of the earth. They are a farming oriented country, and most people are farmers. They place emphasis on making the most of the space that you have since they are located on an island with limited space. Their earth powers allow them to build multi-story farms full of many, many crops.
